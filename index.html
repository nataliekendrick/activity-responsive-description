Candle

The first thing I saw when picking an object was my candle on my desk. I would describe the function of the candle as a way to enhance our sense of smell when entering a room. My candle has a vanilla praline pumpkin scent. The candle consists of having a waxy texture which is covered by a hollow heat-proof glass. In the center of the wax, there is a candlewick. The candlewick is meant to be lit by fire as it will provide the scent of the candle to spread.
